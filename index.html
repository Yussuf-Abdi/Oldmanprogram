APT Corrections
daily priority
🔓
Unlock Hip Flexors
APT keeps hip flexors short and dominant — they pull your pelvis forward and shut off glutes. Release them first.
→ 90/90 Hip Stretch
→ Couch Stretch
→ Low Lunge w/ Reach
→ Reduce sitting time
🍑
Wake Up the Glutes
Overactive hip flexors = inhibited glutes. If glutes are asleep, quads and lower back compensate — weak drives, knee stress.
→ Glute Bridge pre-lift
→ Banded Clamshell
→ Hip Thrust
→ Single-leg Glute Bridge
🧱
Anti-Extension Core
APT = weak lower abs and deep stabilizers. Train exercises that resist the pelvis from tipping forward. Transfers to stopping hard.
→ Dead Bug
→ Hollow Body Hold
→ Pallof Press
→ Ab Wheel (phase 2+)
⚡
APT & Your First Step
Anterior tilt limits hip extensor range — limits how far glutes can fire into each step. Fix APT = more power transfer per stride.
→ Before EVERY workout: 90/90 + glute bridge
→ After EVERY workout: couch stretch
